What We Do

Our logistics have a huge field-leading web based front end, all the latest in web technology is used to provide a high quality service for our clientele. Our main users are corporate consumers, and unfortunately our shipping is not available for an individual. Our high-end database allows multiple demographics from the clientele and our own employees to have different views of the database. Our own employees, on the logistics side are able to access views of the depots locations, and cargo/goods information. Our accountancy employees are able to see views of the transactions, contracts, staff details, banking information and orders. For our clients, views of the services we provide will be available, such as our different priorities of shipping, our cheapest options or shipping with tracking information.
